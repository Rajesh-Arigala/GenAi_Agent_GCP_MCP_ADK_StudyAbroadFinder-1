- **Location:** Pittsburgh, USA
- **Ranking:** Top university for Machine Learning
- **Why recommended:** Consistently ranked among the top for Machine Learning, renowned for its School of Computer Science.
- **Scholarships available:**
    - NSF Graduate Research Fellowship: $37,000/year, for US citizens and residents.
    - Google PhD Fellowship: Full tuition + $40,000 stipend, for PhD students.
    - CMU School of CS Fellowship: $30,000/year, for admitted CMU students.
- **Monthly cost of living breakdown (Pittsburgh):** Data not available through the tool.
- **Visa type and key requirements (USA):** F-1 Student Visa, 3-5 weeks processing time, $510 application fee. Work permit: 20 hrs/week on campus; CPT/OPT for off-campus. Schedule interview early.

- **Nearby places of interest:** Phipps Conservatory and Botanical Gardens, Carnegie Museum of Art.
